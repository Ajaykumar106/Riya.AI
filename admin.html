About Riya.AI

Riya.AI is India's first truly personal AI friend — built by a 20-year-old solo developer from scratch.

Not a chatbot. Not an assistant. A friend who understands Indian culture, speaks Hinglish naturally, and genuinely remembers you.

Built with: Firebase + Gemini 2.0 Flash + Groq + ElevenLabs + Vercel + Render
